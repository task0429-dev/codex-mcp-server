# Zero Budget Marketing Engine - 30 Day Execution Plan

## Command Intent

Build a zero-paid-budget growth engine that compounds through content, SEO, outbound, partnerships, lead magnets, and disciplined follow-up.

## Success Criteria

- A working weekly content engine is live.
- Lead capture and attribution are measurable.
- At least one repeatable outbound workflow is active.
- Task Enterprise has operating dashboards for traffic, leads, reply rate, and booked calls.
- The system can continue running with agent ownership rather than ad spend.

## Phase Map

### Days 1-7

- Audit channels, offers, assets, analytics, CRM tags, and capture paths.
- Lock ICP, value proposition, and messaging pillars.
- Build content calendar and first lead magnet.
- Stand up tracking, email capture, and first welcome sequence.

### Days 8-14

- Begin community and partnership outreach.
- Publish SEO and authority content.
- Push social distribution and repurposing cadence.
- Review week 2 results and adjust landing and offer messaging.

### Days 15-21

- Ship second lead magnet and referral mechanics.
- Grow newsletter and authority content loops.
- Expand audio, LinkedIn, and resource-led reach.
- Prune low-yield channels.

### Days 22-30

- Run webinar or live event.
- Execute re-engagement and conversion optimization.
- Submit to directories, ship a free resource, and tighten PR/media outreach.
- Consolidate winning channels into a repeatable operating system.

## Week 1 Focus Blocks

1. Asset and channel audit
2. ICP and offer definition
3. Tracking and CRM tag setup
4. Content calendar setup
5. Lead magnet and landing path
6. Email automation
7. Weekly review and adjustment

## Reporting Rhythm

- Daily: owner progress and blocker capture
- Weekly: metrics review and channel prioritization
- End of 30 days: what to scale, automate, and retire
